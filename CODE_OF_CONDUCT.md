# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical facts and reproducible behavior.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or PRs that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in a ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report incidents to: `wintopic@proton.me`.
